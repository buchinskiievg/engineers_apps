Voltage Drop Calculator — LV & MV up to 33 kV

Multi-feeder, multi-segment voltage drop per IEC 60364-5-52 Annex D (LV) and IEC 60502-2 / IEC 60287 (MV, up to 33 kV inclusive).
Segments can mix single-phase, three-phase, and DC on the same bus. All computation runs in your browser — no data leaves the page.
